objections, handled.

"can't this be gamed?"
any single signal can. that's the point of showing all of them and never handing out a verdict. gaming one signal is visible against the others.
"why won't meta just build it?"
they protect their own platform, not journalism. their incentive is labeling their feed, not giving a rival's content a checkable proof. cross-platform and reader-facing is the gap they won't fill.
"what's the moat?"
the badge network and the growing proof corpus. embeds compound. each one is a link back.
"who pays first?"
newsrooms with legal and credibility exposure. then brands and platforms facing the same provenance regulation.
